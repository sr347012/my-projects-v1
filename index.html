    Twilio Integration

    Twilio is a service provider who provides support on video calling, audio calling, chat application etc., We integrated the video into core Launchpad to provide the customer a foresight on the loan process. When a loan officer calls a customer for loan application verification, we provide live video support inorder to enhance the customer's experience. My roles and responsibilities include: To add the video feature using Angular and Twilio REST APIs. To provide the customer an end-to-end experience about the loan process. To add the programmable video using Angular into the existing application To explore the various options of integrating video with the launchpad application To present the client about salient features and provide the demo.
